## Purpose
Write a complete, high-converting Video Sales Letter script using RMBC Brief as the blueprint.

## Prerequisite
- RMBC Brief complete (build-rmbc-brief.md)
- Awareness stage and mechanism confirmed

## Execution Steps

### Section 1: Hook (0-90 seconds)
Goal: Stop the scroll. Make them keep watching.
- Open mid-scene or with provocative statement
- NEVER open with name or company
- Create immediate identification with avatar
- Promise the video is worth their time
- Target length: 150-250 words

### Section 2: Problem Agitation (10-20% of VSL)
Goal: Make the problem feel real, urgent, personal
- Confirm they have the problem
- Validate frustration ("you've probably tried...")
- Agitate consequences of NOT solving it
- BLAME REMOVAL: "It's not your fault because..."
- Introduce root cause (but not yet the solution)

### Section 3: Mechanism Revelation (20-30% of VSL)
Goal: Show them why everything else failed and why THIS is different
- Failed solutions section (empathetically acknowledge what they've tried)
- Root cause reveal (the real reason other solutions don't work)
- Discovery story (credible story of how mechanism was found)
- Mechanism explanation (simple, visual, specific)
- Why this time is different (mechanism addresses root cause)

### Section 4: Proof Stack (20-25% of VSL)
Goal: Make it believable before the ask
Build in this order:
1. Scientific proof (most credible — studies, research)
2. Authority proof (expert credentials, endorsements)
3. Social proof (testimonials — specific: name, location, result)
4. Demonstration (if available)

### Section 5: Offer Presentation (15-20% of VSL)
Goal: Make buying feel inevitable
- Introduce the product (name it NOW — not before)
- Component/ingredient breakdown with benefits
- Bonus stack (add value before price)
- Price reveal with anchoring
- Guarantee (risk reversal)
- Urgency/scarcity with real reason

### Section 6: Close and CTA (5-10% of VSL)
Goal: Make action obvious and easy
- Summarize transformation
- Clear, specific CTA (what to do RIGHT NOW)
- Repeat urgency
- Final emotional push (life with solution vs. without)
- Reiterate guarantee

## Quality Standards
- Hook: Opens with scene or controversy — NOT with name/company
- Blame removal: Present in problem section
- Mechanism: Specific enough to be credible, simple enough to understand
- Proof: At minimum 2 testimonials with name + location + specific result
- Scarcity: Real deadline with explained reason
- CTA: Single, clear action step

## Veto Conditions
- VETO if opening is with company name or 'Hi, my name is...'
- VETO if mechanism is vague
- VETO if price is revealed before proof stack
- VETO if testimonials lack specifics
- VETO if no blame removal present
