# Task: Write Headlines

**Agent:** gary-halbert
**Tier:** 2
**Type:** Creation
**Elicit:** true

## Purpose
Generate 25 headline options and select the strongest for testing.

## Inputs Required
- Product and market
- Awareness stage (1-5)
- Primary desire/fear of the starving crowd
- Mechanism (if Stage 3+)

## Execution Steps

### Round 1 (Headlines 1-8): Obvious Variants
Direct benefit headlines. Expected — get these out first.

### Round 2 (Headlines 9-16): Question and Curiosity
How-to, question, and counterintuitive approaches.

### Round 3 (Headlines 17-25): Gold Zone
Specific, unexpected, identity-focused, and mechanism-led.
The best headlines are almost always in this round.

## Headline Formula Bank

**Benefit:**
- How to [Result] in [Time] [Without Sacrifice]
- Now You Can [Desired Result] Without [Feared Cost]
- [Number] Ways to [Result] Starting [Time Frame]

**Question:**
- Are You Making These [Number] Mistakes With [Topic]?
- Do You Have What It Takes to [Aspiration]?
- What Do [Authority Group] Know About [Topic] That You Don't?

**Counterintuitive:**
- Why [Common Advice] Is Wrong About [Topic]
- The Surprising Reason [Common Belief] Doesn't Work
- Stop Doing [Common Action] If You Want [Result]

**Specific Story:**
- How [Person] [Achieved Result] Without [Sacrifice]
- The Strange Secret a [Surprising Source] Discovered About [Topic]

**Target Qualifier:**
- To Every [Avatar] Who Wants to [Result]
- If You [Qualifying Situation], This Message Is for You

**Mechanism:**
- Discovered: The [New] Reason [Problem Happens] — and the Simple Fix
- New [Type of Discovery] Shows Why [Old Approach] Fails

## Selection Criteria
After 25: pick top 3, then select 1 primary.
Primary winner: most specific + most promise + most targeted at avatar.

## Output
- All 25 headlines listed (numbered)
- Top 3 highlighted with rationale
- Final selection marked with A/B test recommendation if applicable

## Veto Conditions
- VETO if fewer than 20 generated
- VETO if all are benefit-only (need variety)
- VETO if no avatar targeting in any headline

## Completion Criteria
- [ ] 25 headlines generated in 3 rounds
- [ ] Top 3 identified with rationale
- [ ] Final selection made with reasoning
- [ ] A/B recommendation if multiple strong contenders
