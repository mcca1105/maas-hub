# Task: Build RMBC Brief

**Agent:** stefan-georgi
**Tier:** 2
**Type:** Planning
**Elicit:** true

## Purpose
Construct the complete RMBC Brief — the architectural plan that the VSL or sales letter
will be built from. The Brief eliminates writer's block and ensures every section hits
its mark before copy writing begins.

## Prerequisites
- Research phase complete (avatar, market, product research documented)
- Unique mechanism identified (develop-unique-mechanism.md complete)

## Inputs Required
- Research documentation
- Mechanism selection
- Proof inventory (testimonials, studies, endorsements)
- Offer details (product, bonuses, price, guarantee)

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Avatar Crystallization
Define the single avatar this copy speaks to:
- Age, gender, life situation
- What awareness stage (1-5)
- What have they tried and failed?
- What is their PRIMARY fear related to this problem?
- What is their PRIMARY dream outcome?
- What language do THEY use for this problem?
- What do they NOT believe yet (objections)?

### Step 2: Hook Selection
Generate 3 hook options based on awareness stage:
- Option A: [type] — for [reason]
- Option B: [type] — for [reason]
- Option C: [type] — for [reason]
Select primary + backup.

### Step 3: Story Architecture
Map the narrative structure:
- Who is the hero? (relatable character — often a patient/customer, sometimes the creator)
- What is their opening struggle? (life before)
- What moment of discovery? (how mechanism was found)
- What transformation happened? (life after)
- What credibility supports this? (credentials, track record)

### Step 4: Proof Inventory
Catalog available proof by type:
- Scientific studies: [list]
- Expert endorsements: [list]
- Testimonials (with specifics): [list]
- Demonstration potential: [yes/no]
- Track record data: [list]

### Step 5: Offer Architecture
Define complete offer:
- Core product: [description]
- Bonuses: [list with values]
- Price: [amount + framing]
- Guarantee: [terms + length]
- Urgency/scarcity: [mechanism + reason]

### Step 6: Objection Inventory
List top 5 objections and planned responses:
1. Objection: [X] → Response: [Y]
2. Objection: [X] → Response: [Y]
...

## Output Format
Complete RMBC Brief document with all sections filled. This becomes the input for write-vsl.md or write-sales-letter.md.

## Veto Conditions
- VETO if avatar is not a single, specific person
- VETO if hook options are not matched to awareness stage
- VETO if proof inventory has fewer than 3 elements
- VETO if objection handling is incomplete (minimum 3 objections addressed)
- VETO if mechanism is not integrated into story architecture

## Completion Criteria
- [ ] Avatar crystallized (specific individual, not demographic)
- [ ] 3 hook options generated with rationale
- [ ] Story architecture mapped
- [ ] Proof inventory cataloged
- [ ] Complete offer defined
- [ ] Top 5 objections and responses documented
- [ ] Brief ready for copy writing phase
