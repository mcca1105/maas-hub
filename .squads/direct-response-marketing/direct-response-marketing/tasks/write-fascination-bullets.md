# Task: Write Fascination Bullets

**Agent:** gary-halbert
**Tier:** 2
**Type:** Creation
**Elicit:** false

## Purpose
Generate 50 fascination bullets, select the best 15-20 for use in sales letters or landing pages.

## Inputs Required
- Product topic and content details
- Target avatar
- Key facts, counterintuitions, surprises about the topic

## What Makes a Fascination (vs. Feature Bullet)

**Feature bullet (WRONG):**
• Contains B12 for energy support

**Fascination bullet (RIGHT):**
• The one vitamin 94% of Americans are deficient in — and why your doctor isn't testing for it even though it's the most common cause of unexplained fatigue

A fascination creates a KNOWLEDGE GAP. The reader MUST have the answer.

## Fascination Formulas

- The [counterintuitive thing] about [topic] that [result]
- Why [common belief] is WRONG — and what the research actually shows
- The [number] [thing] that [specific result] — most [audience] never discovers this
- What [authority] NEVER tells you about [topic] — and why
- The [specific surprising fact] — and what it means for your [situation]
- How to [result] without [feared sacrifice] — using this [specific method]
- The [counterintuitive approach] that [authority/group] uses that nobody talks about
- Warning: [common practice] that INCREASES [negative outcome]
- The [time/amount] secret that [amazing claim] — page [X] inside

## Execution

### Step 1: Research Dump
List every interesting, surprising, counterintuitive, or little-known fact about the topic.
Minimum 60 raw facts. More is better.

### Step 2: Convert Facts to Fascinationss
For each interesting fact, write a fascination that:
- Reveals the tension (what they don't know that they should)
- Withholds the full answer (the product delivers it)
- Uses specific words when possible

### Step 3: Cull and Polish
Remove:
- Anything that feels like a feature
- Duplicates
- Anything that gives away the full answer
Polish top candidates.

### Step 4: Order by Strength
Rank 1-25 (from strongest curiosity-creating to good).
Select top 15-20 for deployment.

## Quality Standards
- Every bullet creates a knowledge gap — reader MUST find the answer
- No bullet is a feature description
- At least 5 bullets use specific numbers
- At least 5 bullets are counterintuitive

## Veto Conditions
- VETO if fewer than 30 generated
- VETO if bullets are feature descriptions
- VETO if bullets give away the full answer

## Completion Criteria
- [ ] 50 raw fascinations generated
- [ ] Best 15-20 selected with rationale
- [ ] No feature bullets in final selection
- [ ] All create knowledge gaps
