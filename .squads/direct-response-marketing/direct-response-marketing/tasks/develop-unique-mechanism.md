# Task: Develop Unique Mechanism

**Agent:** stefan-georgi
**Tier:** 2
**Type:** Strategic
**Elicit:** true

## Purpose
Identify and articulate the Unique Mechanism — the specific, credible reason WHY this product
produces results when other approaches have failed. Required for Stage 3+ markets.

## Prerequisite
- Awareness stage diagnosis complete (diagnose-awareness-stage.md)
- Stage must be 3 or higher to require mechanism
- Product/ingredient/process documentation available

## Inputs Required
- Product details (ingredients, process, technology)
- Target avatar and their prior failed solutions
- What competitors claim (to ensure differentiation)
- Any scientific research or studies

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Root Cause Analysis
Identify WHY the prospect's prior solutions failed:
- What did they try? (list solutions)
- Why didn't it work? (the real reason, not 'they weren't committed')
- What is the ACTUAL root cause the problem?
- What are competitors addressing? (usually the symptom)

### Step 2: Mechanism Candidate Generation
Generate 3-5 mechanism candidates based on product's actual differentiators:

For each candidate, answer:
1. What specifically does it do? (biological/mechanical process)
2. Why is it different from what competitors do?
3. Can it be explained simply? (2-3 sentences to a layperson)
4. Is there any research/evidence supporting it?
5. Does it explain why OTHER solutions failed?

### Step 3: Mechanism Scoring
Score each candidate (1-5):
- Specificity: How precise and credible is it?
- Novelty: How new/different is it?
- Simplicity: Can a layperson understand it?
- Evidence: Is there proof it works?
- Differentiation: Does it explain competitor failure?

Select highest-scoring mechanism.

### Step 4: Mechanism Statement Development
Write the mechanism in 3 forms:
1. **Technical** (for authority sections): Full scientific explanation
2. **Simplified** (for main copy): Layperson-accessible, visual
3. **Headline** (for hooks): Compressed, curiosity-generating

### Step 5: Failed Solutions Bridge
Write the transition:
'[Common solution] doesn't work because [root cause they missed].
[Mechanism name] works because it addresses [root cause directly].'

## Output Format
```
UNIQUE MECHANISM REPORT

Product: [name]
Market Stage: [3/4/5]

ROOT CAUSE IDENTIFIED:
[What's actually causing the problem that competitors aren't addressing]

MECHANISM SELECTED: [Name of mechanism]
Type: [ingredient / process / technology / reversal / elimination]

MECHANISM STATEMENT (Simplified):
[2-3 sentences — layperson accessible]

MECHANISM STATEMENT (Technical):
[Full explanation for authority section]

MECHANISM HEADLINE:
[Curiosity-generating compressed version for hooks]

FAILED SOLUTIONS BRIDGE:
[The paragraph that transitions from competitor failures to your mechanism]

EVIDENCE INVENTORY:
- [Study/proof 1]
- [Study/proof 2]
```

## Veto Conditions
- VETO if mechanism is vague ('natural formula', 'proprietary blend') — must be specific
- VETO if mechanism is the same as competitors' primary claim
- VETO if mechanism cannot be explained in 3 sentences
- VETO if there's no differentiation from prior solutions

## Completion Criteria
- [ ] Root cause of problem identified
- [ ] 3+ mechanism candidates generated
- [ ] Candidates scored and one selected
- [ ] Mechanism stated in 3 forms (technical, simplified, headline)
- [ ] Failed solutions bridge written
- [ ] Evidence inventory documented
