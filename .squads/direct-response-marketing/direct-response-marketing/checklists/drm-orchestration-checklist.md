# Checklist: DRM Squad Orchestration (Launch Readiness)

**Use when:** Final review before launching a complete marketing campaign or funnel.
**Agent:** drm-chief

---

## Section 1: Strategic Foundation

- [ ] Customer avatar is documented as ONE specific person (not demographic)
- [ ] Eugene Schwartz awareness stage is assigned with evidence
- [ ] Unique mechanism is named and differentiated from competitors
- [ ] Offer passes the no-brainer 4-question test
- [ ] RMBC brief is complete (Research + Mechanism + Brief + Copy)

## Section 2: Message Consistency (Message Match Audit)

- [ ] Ad headline → Squeeze page headline → VSL/Sales letter headline all reinforce same promise
- [ ] Mechanism described in ads matches mechanism described in sales page
- [ ] Testimonials used in ads match audience shown in ads
- [ ] Email subject lines align with the content of the emails
- [ ] No message contradiction anywhere in the funnel

## Section 3: Copy Asset Completion

- [ ] All funnel pages have copy (no placeholder text)
- [ ] Primary conversion asset (VSL or sales letter) is complete and reviewed
- [ ] Email sequences are loaded in ESP
- [ ] Ad copy is approved and uploaded to platform(s)
- [ ] Post-purchase sequences (upsell scripts, onboarding emails) are complete

## Section 4: Persuasion Coverage

- [ ] All 7 Cialdini principles are represented in the funnel
- [ ] Top 5 objections are handled somewhere in the funnel
- [ ] Guarantee is specific, named, and positioned near CTA
- [ ] Urgency mechanism is genuine and enforcement is confirmed
- [ ] Price anchor is established before price is revealed on every page

## Section 5: Technical Readiness

- [ ] All pages load in under 3 seconds on mobile
- [ ] All links tested and working
- [ ] Conversion tracking confirmed (pixel firing on correct events)
- [ ] UTM parameters on all ad links
- [ ] Split test structure loaded (at minimum: ad hooks in test)
- [ ] Kill conditions defined for paid traffic

## Section 6: Proof & Compliance

- [ ] Testimonials have written permission documented
- [ ] Income/results claims comply with FTC guidelines
- [ ] Typical results disclosure present where required
- [ ] Privacy policy and terms of service linked on opt-in pages
- [ ] Unsubscribe works in all email systems

## Section 7: Post-Launch Monitoring Plan

- [ ] Defined check-in schedule (day 1, day 3, day 7, day 14)
- [ ] Metrics dashboard set up
- [ ] Alert thresholds configured (CPL too high / open rate too low / page error)
- [ ] Point person assigned for each channel
- [ ] Escalation protocol defined (who decides if campaign pauses)

## Section 8: Optimization Queue

- [ ] Split test #1 is defined and ready to run at launch
- [ ] Priority list of next tests is documented (what to test after first results)
- [ ] Funnel leak diagnosis scheduled for Day 7-14

## Pre-Launch Gate (MUST PASS ALL)

- [ ] Avatar document exists and is specific
- [ ] At least one conversion asset is complete (VSL or sales letter)
- [ ] At least one email sequence is loaded (5+ emails)
- [ ] At least 3 ad variations exist for paid traffic
- [ ] Tracking is confirmed working
- [ ] No broken links exist in funnel

**If any Pre-Launch Gate item is unchecked → HALT LAUNCH until resolved.**

## Veto Conditions

- VETO if message match fails between any two consecutive funnel steps
- VETO if no tracking on conversion events
- VETO if urgency mechanism is fake (no enforcement plan)
- VETO if any Pre-Launch Gate item is unchecked
- VETO if proof contains claims that can't be substantiated
