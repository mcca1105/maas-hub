# Pre-Write Research Checklist
**Agent:** `@drm:stefan-georgi`
**Purpose:** Confirm Research phase is complete before moving to Mechanism
**Standard:** Stefan Georgi RMBC Method — Research Phase Gate
**Run:** At the end of Research phase, before writing the Mechanism

---

## 🔴 CRITICAL — Research Completeness Gate

### Prospect Research
- [ ] Avatar name and age range documented
- [ ] Avatar's primary frustration captured in their OWN words (VOC)
- [ ] At least 3 VOC sources reviewed:
  - [ ] Amazon reviews (1-star and 5-star)
  - [ ] Reddit / forums
  - [ ] Customer interviews, surveys, or direct conversations
- [ ] Minimum 20 exact prospect phrases collected (their language, not yours)
- [ ] Previous failed solutions documented (what they tried and why it didn't work)
- [ ] Dream outcome documented in specific, vivid terms

**Research verdict:** DO I truly know what this prospect is thinking? YES / NO

### Market Research
- [ ] Awareness stage confirmed (Schwartz Stage 1-5): Stage _____
- [ ] Traffic temperature confirmed: Cold / Warm / Hot
- [ ] Top 3 competitors' mechanisms documented (what are they already saying?)
- [ ] Market sophistication level determined (Schwartz Level 1-5): Level _____
- [ ] White space identified (what's NOT being said that prospects want to hear)

**White space summary:**
```
_______________________________________________________________
```

### Product Research
- [ ] Every ingredient / component documented with its function
- [ ] Scientific or clinical support for core claims identified
- [ ] Strongest provable claim identified: ___________________
- [ ] Origin story of the product/formula researched (who made it, why)
- [ ] Usage instructions understood (how to use, how quickly it works)

### Proof Research
- [ ] Best testimonials collected and organized
- [ ] Any clinical studies, clinical trials, or peer-reviewed research found
- [ ] Before-and-after documentation available? Yes / No
- [ ] Endorsement or expert affiliation available? Yes / No

---

## 🟡 HIGH PRIORITY — Language Readiness

### VOC Language Bank
Before moving to Mechanism, confirm language bank is populated:

**Pain Language (10 minimum):**
- [ ] Exact phrases describing the problem
- [ ] Emotional words they use (embarrassed, frustrated, hopeless)
- [ ] Consequences they describe ("I can't even...", "I miss...")

**Desire Language (5 minimum):**
- [ ] What they say they want most
- [ ] The dream outcome in their words
- [ ] What would change in their life if this were solved

**Failed Attempts Language (5 minimum):**
- [ ] Names of things they've tried
- [ ] Why each failed in their words
- [ ] The specific disappointment ("I was so hopeful but then...")

**Purchase/Skepticism Language:**
- [ ] What almost stopped them from buying
- [ ] What convinced them (from existing buyer interviews)

---

## 🟢 BEST PRACTICE — Depth Check

### Research Quality Markers
- [ ] I could write a 500-word paragraph describing this prospect from memory (no notes)
- [ ] I know 3 specific reasons why previous solutions failed for them
- [ ] I can name their dominant emotion right now with confidence
- [ ] The white space opportunity is clear and specific
- [ ] I've spent at least 8-10 hours in research (not just 1-2)

---

## ❌ AUTO-FAIL CONDITIONS — Do Not Proceed to Mechanism If:

- [ ] ❌ Prospect language is vague ("they're frustrated") vs. specific (exact phrases)
- [ ] ❌ Only 1 VOC source reviewed (Amazon reviews alone is not enough)
- [ ] ❌ Awareness stage is still unclear
- [ ] ❌ Competitor mechanisms are unknown (flying blind on differentiation)
- [ ] ❌ Research time was under 3 hours for a new-to-you market
- [ ] ❌ No proof elements identified (testimonials, studies, data — at least one type)
