# Checklist: Email Sequence Quality

**Use when:** Reviewing a completed email sequence before loading into ESP.

---

## Section 1: Subject Lines

- [ ] Every email has a subject line under 60 characters
- [ ] No spam trigger words (FREE, URGENT, CLICK HERE, WINNER, GUARANTEED)
- [ ] Subject lines use at least 3 different formula types across the sequence
- [ ] Each subject line stands alone — makes sense without reading the email
- [ ] A/B test variant subject line written for every email

## Section 2: Email Body — Structure

- [ ] Every email has a hook in the first line (no slow warmup openers)
- [ ] Every email has a story element (Frank Kern rule)
- [ ] Every email delivers a piece of genuine value (not pure promotion)
- [ ] Every email has one clear CTA (not multiple competing links)
- [ ] Email length is appropriate: 200-400 words for promo, 400-600 for value emails

## Section 3: Conversion Mechanics

- [ ] CTA link appears at least twice in emails over 400 words
- [ ] CTA is specific about what to do ("Click here to download" vs "Click here")
- [ ] Social proof present in at least 1 email per sequence
- [ ] Objection handling present in at least 1 email
- [ ] Urgency/deadline in final emails if campaign has an expiry

## Section 4: Tone and Voice

- [ ] Emails sound like 1:1 conversation, not corporate broadcast
- [ ] Avatar's language and vocabulary used throughout
- [ ] No passive voice in first 3 lines of any email
- [ ] "You" appears more than "I/we" in every email
- [ ] Emails would survive the "bar test" (sounds like something you'd say to a friend)

## Section 5: Technical

- [ ] Preview text (preheader) written for every email
- [ ] Unsubscribe link confirmed in every email
- [ ] All links tested and working
- [ ] UTM parameters added to all links
- [ ] Plain text version matches HTML version

## Section 6: Sequence Logic

- [ ] Emails flow as a connected narrative (not disconnected standalone messages)
- [ ] Buyer segment excluded from purchase-prompting emails
- [ ] Re-engagement branch exists for non-openers
- [ ] Sequence doesn't go more than 48 hours without an email during a launch
- [ ] First email delivers promised lead magnet or welcome value within 5 minutes

## Veto Conditions

- VETO if any email has no CTA link
- VETO if welcome email is over 400 words (first email must be short and fast)
- VETO if "just checking in" subject line appears (lazy and ineffective)
- VETO if any email sends buyers the same purchase prompt as non-buyers
