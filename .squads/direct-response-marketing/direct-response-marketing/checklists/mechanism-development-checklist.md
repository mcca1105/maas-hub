# Mechanism Development Checklist
**Agent:** `@drm:stefan-georgi`
**Purpose:** Validate that a mechanism is strong enough to carry a VSL or sales letter
**Standard:** Stefan Georgi RMBC Method — Mechanism Phase
**Run:** After Research phase, before writing the Brief

---

## 🔴 CRITICAL — Mechanism Foundation

### The Core Question
Before anything else, answer:
> "Why does this product work when everything else the prospect has tried has failed?"

The answer IS the mechanism. Write it here:
```
_______________________________________________________________
_______________________________________________________________
```

### Existence Check
- [ ] A specific mechanism has been identified (not just benefits)
- [ ] The mechanism explains WHY the product works, not just WHAT it does
- [ ] The mechanism directly addresses why previous solutions failed
- [ ] The mechanism is grounded in logic, science, or research (not invented)

### Specificity Check
- [ ] The mechanism is specific enough to be unique
- [ ] It names a specific compound, process, pathway, or system (not vague)
- [ ] A competitor could NOT use this exact mechanism for their product
- [ ] The mechanism can be explained in under 60 seconds

## 🟡 HIGH PRIORITY — Mechanism Quality

### The Villain Test
A strong mechanism requires a "villain" — the hidden root cause the prospect hasn't addressed.
- [ ] The villain has been identified: ___________________
- [ ] The villain is believable (logical, scientific, or historically documented)
- [ ] The villain explains the prospect's past failures clearly
- [ ] The villain is something the prospect hasn't heard blamed before

**Complete this sentence:**
> "The real reason [problem persists] is not [common belief]. It's actually [villain/root cause]."
```
_______________________________________________________________
```

### Naming the Mechanism
- [ ] Mechanism has been given a proprietary name
- [ ] Name format: [Adjective] [Noun] [System/Protocol/Formula/Method]
- [ ] Name is memorable and easy to repeat
- [ ] Name hasn't been used by a competitor (basic Google check done)

**Mechanism Name:** ___________________

### Proof Scaffolding
- [ ] At least one piece of evidence supports the mechanism
  - [ ] Peer-reviewed study
  - [ ] Expert quote with credentials
  - [ ] Historical/documented case
  - [ ] Logic chain (A causes B causes C — demonstrably)
  - [ ] Visual demonstration possible
- [ ] Proof source is cited: ___________________
- [ ] Proof directly supports this specific mechanism (not tangentially)

---

## 🟢 BEST PRACTICE — Mechanism Strength

### Market Differentiation
- [ ] Top 3 competitors' mechanisms listed:
  1. ___________________
  2. ___________________
  3. ___________________
- [ ] Our mechanism is categorically different from all three
- [ ] Mechanism creates a new category, not just a better product

### Copy Integration
- [ ] Mechanism can be introduced in a single dramatic sentence
- [ ] Mechanism can be expanded across 2-5 minutes of VSL or 3-5 pages of copy
- [ ] The mechanism name will be memorable after one hearing
- [ ] The mechanism creates "aha moment" — prospect sees their past failures differently

### The Before/After Frame
Complete the mechanism story arc:
```
BEFORE (why they failed):
The prospect failed because [previous solutions] only addressed [symptom/surface issue].
They never tackled [villain/root cause].

AFTER (why this works):
[Mechanism Name] works by [specific process], which directly addresses [root cause].
This is why [product] produces [result] even when [other solutions] failed.
```

---

## ❌ AUTO-FAIL CONDITIONS

- [ ] ❌ Mechanism is just a benefit reframed ("makes you lose weight faster")
- [ ] ❌ Mechanism is so technical the average person can't follow it
- [ ] ❌ Mechanism has no evidence of any kind behind it
- [ ] ❌ Mechanism is identical to a competitor's primary claim
- [ ] ❌ Mechanism doesn't explain why previous solutions failed
- [ ] ❌ Mechanism has no proprietary name (generic = forgettable = low conversion)
