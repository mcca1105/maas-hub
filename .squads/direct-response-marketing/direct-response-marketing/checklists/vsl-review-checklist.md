# VSL Review Checklist
**Agent:** @vsl-master
**Purpose:** Pre-flight validation before VSL production
**Standard:** Stefan Georgi RMBC Method

---

## 🔴 CRITICAL (MUST PASS - Non-negotiable)

### Research Foundation
- [ ] **30+ hours of research completed** (Amazon reviews, forums, surveys)
- [ ] **Customer language documented** (exact phrases, pain points captured)
- [ ] **3+ competitor VSLs analyzed** (what worked, what didn't)
- [ ] **Unique mechanism identified** (clear differentiation from competition)

### Structure (RMBC)
- [ ] **Big Idea is compelling** (memorable, counterintuitive, transformative)
- [ ] **Pattern interrupt present** (0-15 sec - stops attention)
- [ ] **Hook delivers promise** (15-45 sec - specific result + timeframe)
- [ ] **Story arc complete** (3-8 min - relatable character → struggle → discovery)
- [ ] **Mechanism revealed** (2-5 min - how it works, why different)
- [ ] **Offer clearly presented** (3-5 min - value stack visible)
- [ ] **Close with urgency** (1-2 min - scarcity/deadline)

### CTA Placement (CRITICAL - 80% of conversion)
- [ ] **70% CTA present** (soft ask - "Imagine if...")
- [ ] **85% CTA present** ⚠️ **MAIN CTA** (after offer - "Click below now")
- [ ] **95% CTA present** (urgency reminder - "Only X hours left")
- [ ] **100% CTA present** (final call - "Don't miss out")

### Emotional Arc
- [ ] **Hope** (hook - there's a solution!)
- [ ] **Empathy** (story start - I understand your pain)
- [ ] **Frustration** (struggle - nothing worked)
- [ ] **Curiosity** (discovery - what changed?)
- [ ] **Excitement** (mechanism - this is different!)
- [ ] **Desire** (offer - I need this)
- [ ] **Urgency** (scarcity - I need this NOW)

---

## 🟡 HIGH PRIORITY (Should Pass)

### Timing
- [ ] **Total length: 15-25 minutes** (optimal for conversion)
- [ ] **Pattern interrupt: 0-15 seconds**
- [ ] **Hook: 15-45 seconds**
- [ ] **Story: 3-8 minutes**
- [ ] **Mechanism: 2-5 minutes**
- [ ] **Offer: 3-5 minutes**
- [ ] **Close: 1-2 minutes**
- [ ] **Breathing room every 3-5 minutes** (pause for processing)

### Proof Elements
- [ ] **Testimonials at 40%** (mid-script social proof)
- [ ] **Testimonials at 75%** (pre-offer reinforcement)
- [ ] **Scientific backing** (if applicable - studies, data)
- [ ] **Guarantee at 85%** (risk reversal before final CTA)

### Production Notes
- [ ] **Works WITHOUT sound** (captions carry full message)
- [ ] **B-roll suggestions documented** (every 30-45 seconds)
- [ ] **Text overlay moments marked** (key stats, quotes)
- [ ] **Visual transitions noted** (smooth flow)

---

## 🟢 BEST PRACTICE (Nice to Have)

### Advanced Techniques
- [ ] **Open loops created** (curiosity throughout)
- [ ] **Pattern interrupts every 2-3 min** (maintain attention)
- [ ] **Specificity in claims** (numbers, timeframes, details)
- [ ] **Future pacing** (imagine yourself with result)
- [ ] **Objection handling** (address "yeah, but..." moments)

### Copy Quality
- [ ] **Conversational tone** (sounds like talking, not reading)
- [ ] **Active voice predominant** (you will, not you can)
- [ ] **Sensory language** (feel, see, hear, taste, touch)
- [ ] **Contrast used** (before vs after, old way vs new way)

---

## ❌ COMMON MISTAKES (Auto-Fail if Present)

### Structure Errors
- [ ] ❌ **Mechanism revealed too early** (kills curiosity)
- [ ] ❌ **No emotional journey** (flat = boring = scroll)
- [ ] ❌ **Talking about product instead of transformation**
- [ ] ❌ **Missing main CTA at 85%** (single biggest conversion killer)

### Offer Errors
- [ ] ❌ **No value stack** (just price = low conversion)
- [ ] ❌ **No urgency/scarcity** (why buy now?)
- [ ] ❌ **Weak or missing guarantee** (-30% conversion)
- [ ] ❌ **Too many offers** (dilutes focus)

### Production Errors
- [ ] ❌ **Doesn't work without sound** (80% watch muted!)
- [ ] ❌ **No captions** (accessibility + conversion killer)
- [ ] ❌ **Too long (>30 min)** (drop-off cliff)
- [ ] ❌ **Too short (<12 min)** (insufficient selling)

## 🎯 STEFAN GEORGI'S GOLD STANDARD

> "Your VSL has ONE job: take the prospect on an emotional journey where buying is the only logical conclusion. If your research is weak, your VSL will be weak. If your big idea isn't compelling, nothing else matters. And if you don't have a CTA at 85%, you're leaving 40% of conversions on the table."

**Key Metrics (Target):**
- Conversion Rate: **3-8%** (cold traffic)
- Average View Duration: **60%+**
- CTA Click Rate: **15%+**
- Drop-off Points: **<10% before 85%**
