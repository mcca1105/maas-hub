# Retargeting Campaign Checklist
**Agent:** `@drm:frank-kern`
**Purpose:** Validate retargeting campaign before launch
**Standard:** Frank Kern BDR + Cialdini Commitment & Consistency
**Run:** Before activating retargeting ads for any funnel

---

## 🔴 CRITICAL — Audience & Intent Segmentation

### Pixel/Tracking Setup
- [ ] Pixel or tracking code fires on every key page:
  - [ ] Landing/opt-in page (visited but didn't opt-in)
  - [ ] Opt-in confirmation (opted-in but didn't buy)
  - [ ] Sales page (visited but didn't buy)
  - [ ] Checkout page (started checkout but didn't complete)
  - [ ] Thank-you/confirmation page (purchased — EXCLUDE from most retargeting)
- [ ] Custom conversions confirmed firing correctly (test before launching)
- [ ] Buyer audience created and excluded from prospect retargeting

### Audience Segments Created (Priority Order)
- [ ] **Segment 1 — Cart Abandoners:** Visited checkout, didn't purchase (hottest)
- [ ] **Segment 2 — Sales Page Visitors:** Saw offer, didn't buy
- [ ] **Segment 3 — Video Watchers:** Watched 50%+ of VSL or video
- [ ] **Segment 4 — Lead Magnet Downloaders:** Got freebie, didn't buy
- [ ] **Segment 5 — General Site Visitors:** Visited any page (coldest retargeting)
- [ ] Separate ad sets with separate messaging for each segment

---

## 🟡 HIGH PRIORITY — Message Matching

### Segment 1: Cart Abandoners
- [ ] Ad acknowledges they were "just about to" (uses their behavior)
- [ ] Removes friction: "Still thinking about it? Here's your answer to [objection]"
- [ ] May offer bonus or guarantee reminder (not discount — devalues offer)
- [ ] Urgency element if applicable (honest deadline)

### Segment 2: Sales Page Visitors
- [ ] Ad reinforces the strongest mechanism or proof element
- [ ] Testimonial format works well here (social proof)
- [ ] Addresses most common objection this stage has
- [ ] Links back to sales page (not home page)

### Segment 3: Video Watchers (50%+)
- [ ] Ad references content they watched ("If you watched [video]...")
- [ ] Continues the story — gives them the next piece of information
- [ ] Soft CTA (invites back to full VSL or offers a related piece of content)

### Segment 4: Lead Magnet Downloaders
- [ ] Ad shows the next step: "You downloaded [lead magnet], now see the full system"
- [ ] Bridges from lead magnet topic to Tripwire or Core Offer
- [ ] Does NOT assume they remember the lead magnet (re-establish value)

---

## 🟢 BEST PRACTICE — Creative & Frequency

### Ad Creative by Segment
- [ ] Minimum 3 creative variations per segment (combat ad fatigue)
- [ ] Video and static image both tested per segment
- [ ] Creative clearly different from initial cold traffic ads (they've seen those)
- [ ] Testimonial creatives tested (especially for Segments 2-3)

### Frequency Management
- [ ] Frequency cap set: maximum 3-5 impressions per person per week
- [ ] Ad rotation enabled to prevent single creative burning out
- [ ] 30-day or 60-day window set (not "all time" — staleness reduces conversion)
- [ ] Plan to refresh creative every 14-21 days

### Exclusions
- [ ] Purchasers excluded from all prospect retargeting
- [ ] People who completed upsell excluded from upsell retargeting
- [ ] Unsubscribes excluded (if email is connected to ad platform)
- [ ] High-frequency non-converters excluded after 45 days (wasted spend)

---

## ❌ AUTO-FAIL CONDITIONS

- [ ] ❌ Buyers seeing ads for something they already bought
- [ ] ❌ All segments receiving identical ad creative
- [ ] ❌ No frequency cap (audience gets harassed, brand perception damaged)
- [ ] ❌ Pixel not firing on checkout confirmation (can't exclude buyers)
- [ ] ❌ Retargeting ads link to homepage instead of relevant funnel step
- [ ] ❌ Cart abandoner ads offer a discount unprompted (devalues offer)
