# Checklist: Cold Email Sequence Quality

**Use when:** Reviewing a cold outreach email sequence before sending.

---

## Section 1: Email 1 (First Touch)

- [ ] Email 1 is under 100 words (long cold emails are ignored)
- [ ] Subject line contains the recipient's name or company (personalization)
- [ ] Opening line is about THEM (not about you/your company)
- [ ] Specific relevance is established (why you're emailing THIS person specifically)
- [ ] Single, low-commitment CTA (a question, not "book a call now")
- [ ] Zero attachments (triggers spam, looks pushy)
- [ ] Zero links in Email 1 (triggers spam filters in many systems)

## Section 2: Email 2 (Value Drop)

- [ ] Email 2 delivers genuine value before any ask
- [ ] Value is relevant to their specific business/situation (not generic advice)
- [ ] Value is a specific insight, stat, case study, or tool — not a pitch
- [ ] CTA is still soft (curiosity-based question, not "schedule now")
- [ ] Email 2 references Email 1 naturally (continuation, not restart)

## Section 3: Email 3 (Social Proof / Case Study)

- [ ] Case study is from a similar company/situation to the prospect
- [ ] Case study has specific, measurable result
- [ ] Case study result is directly relevant to what the prospect wants
- [ ] CTA moves slightly warmer ("Would this be relevant for [Company Name]?")
- [ ] Email doesn't lead with "I wanted to follow up on my previous emails"

## Section 4: Email 4 (Break-Up)

- [ ] Break-up email gives them explicit permission to say no
- [ ] Tone is respectful and confident (not needy or passive-aggressive)
- [ ] One final value statement or case study (not just the break-up)
- [ ] CTA is very easy: "Just reply 'not interested' if this isn't a fit"
- [ ] Break-up email is genuinely the last email (no Email 5 after)

## Section 5: Personalization & Research

- [ ] Prospect intelligence collected before writing (company size, role, recent news)
- [ ] Opening line references something specific about them (job post, recent funding, article)
- [ ] Company name is used correctly (not the parent company when you mean the division)
- [ ] Recipient's first name used correctly (no "Hi {first_name}")

## Section 6: Technical & Compliance

- [ ] From name is a real person (not "Marketing Team" or "NoReply")
- [ ] Unsubscribe option available in every email
- [ ] CAN-SPAM / GDPR compliance verified for target market
- [ ] Sending from warmed domain (not brand new domain)
- [ ] Send frequency: at least 2-3 days between touches

## Veto Conditions

- VETO if Email 1 is over 150 words
- VETO if Email 1 is a pitch (not a question or specific insight)
- VETO if all 4 emails are pitch emails with no value
- VETO if sequence sends more than 4 emails (cold prospects aren't warm enough for 8-email sequences)
- VETO if opening line is "I hope this finds you well" or "My name is X and I work at Y"
