## 🔴 CRITICAL — Lead Magnet Foundation

### The Core Promise
- [ ] Lead magnet promises ONE specific outcome (not a collection of tips)
- [ ] Outcome is achievable within 15 minutes or less of consuming it
- [ ] Promise is specific: includes what + timeframe + for whom

**Complete the formula:**
> "[Lead Magnet Name] — [Specific Outcome] for [Target Audience] in [Timeframe]"
```
_______________________________________________________________
```

### Audience Qualification
- [ ] Lead magnet attracts BUYERS, not just freebie-seekers
- [ ] The specific problem addressed requires financial investment to fully solve
- [ ] Someone who wants this for free also wants the paid solution
- [ ] The lead magnet would have sell-able value at a small price point

### Funnel Alignment
- [ ] Lead magnet is directly related to the Tripwire offer
- [ ] Tripwire is the natural "next step" after consuming the lead magnet
- [ ] Lead magnet creates appetite (not full satisfaction) for the paid offer
- [ ] The transition from lead magnet → Tripwire feels logical and natural

---

## 🟡 HIGH PRIORITY — Delivery and Format

### Format Selection
- [ ] Format chosen matches how the audience prefers to consume content:
  - [ ] PDF/Guide — works for reference material, checklists, frameworks
  - [ ] Video training — works for process-based, demonstration content
  - [ ] Quiz — works for diagnosis, personalization needs
  - [ ] Checklist — works for multi-step processes, recurring tasks
  - [ ] Template — works for anything the prospect has to create
  - [ ] Free + Shipping — works for physical products (highest conversion)
- [ ] Format can be consumed in under 15 minutes

### Immediate Value Delivery
- [ ] Lead magnet delivers value even if they never buy anything
- [ ] The content inside is genuinely useful, not a sales brochure
- [ ] At least one quick win can be achieved immediately after consuming it
- [ ] Prospect leaves feeling smarter, more capable, or more confident

### Production Quality
- [ ] Visual design is professional (not amateurish)
- [ ] Content is organized with clear sections/headings
- [ ] No grammatical errors or broken links
- [ ] Mobile-friendly format (PDF readable on phone, video plays on mobile)

---

## 🟢 BEST PRACTICE — Optimization

### Opt-In Page Alignment
- [ ] Opt-in page headline matches lead magnet promise exactly
- [ ] Opt-in form asks for minimal information (email only, or name + email max)
- [ ] Thank-you page / confirmation page delivers the tripwire immediately
- [ ] Delivery is instant (not "check your email in 24 hours")

### Segmentation Value
- [ ] The type of lead magnet selected reveals buyer intent
- [ ] Behavioral tags can be set based on which lead magnet is downloaded
- [ ] Different lead magnets for different awareness stages are mapped out

### Content Seeds
- [ ] Lead magnet references (but doesn't fully deliver) what the paid offer provides
- [ ] At least one natural mention of the paid solution is appropriate within the content
- [ ] The "gap" between lead magnet and full solution is clear (without being pushy)

---

## ❌ AUTO-FAIL CONDITIONS

- [ ] ❌ Lead magnet promises everything (dilutes value and confuses buyers)
- [ ] ❌ Lead magnet is only a sales pitch in disguise (destroys trust)
- [ ] ❌ Lead magnet delivers 100% of what the paid offer delivers (kills conversion)
- [ ] ❌ Lead magnet and Tripwire have no logical connection
- [ ] ❌ Opt-in page asks for phone number without clear reason
- [ ] ❌ Delivery takes more than 5 minutes after opt-in
