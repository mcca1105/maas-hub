## Recommended Stack by Stage

### Starting Out (Budget-friendly)
- ClickFunnels (funnel)
- Canva Pro (creative)
- Google Analytics + Clarity (analytics)
- ActiveCampaign (email)
- Meta + Google Ads (traffic)

### Scaling ($10k-50k/month spend)
- ClickFunnels or Kartra (funnel)
- Adobe Creative Suite (creative)
- GA4 + Hotjar + Hyros (analytics)
- ActiveCampaign (email)
- Multi-platform ads

### Enterprise ($50k+/month spend)
- Custom platform (funnel)
- Full Adobe Suite + design team (creative)
- Hyros + Northbeam + data team (analytics)
- ActiveCampaign or custom (email)
- Full-platform ads + agency
