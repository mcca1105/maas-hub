# SOP: Funnel Audit (Full Diagnostic)

**Method:** Cialdini Persuasion Framework + Halbert Market Hierarchy + Deiss CVO + Schwartz Awareness
**Squad Agents:** `@drm:robert-cialdini` (lead), `@drm:eugene-schwartz` (awareness check), `@drm:ryan-deiss` (CVO structure)
**Estimated Time:** 3-6 hours (complete audit)
**Output:** Funnel Audit Report with ranked issues and specific fixes

---

## When to Use This SOP

- Funnel exists but conversion rate is below target
- Cost per acquisition (CPA) is increasing
- Traffic is coming in but sales are not following
- Client brings an existing funnel and asks "what's wrong?"

---

## Pre-Audit: Gather Data

Before beginning the qualitative audit, collect:

### Traffic Data
```
□ Monthly unique visitors to each funnel step
□ Traffic source breakdown (cold/warm/hot)
□ Traffic source (paid/organic/email/social)
□ Device breakdown (mobile/desktop)
□ Geographic distribution
```

### Conversion Data
```
□ Opt-in conversion rate (goal: 20-40%+ depending on traffic temp)
□ Tripwire conversion rate (goal: 1-5%+ of all traffic, 5-20% of opt-ins)
□ Core offer conversion rate (goal: 1-3%+ of all traffic)
□ Upsell take rate (goal: 15-30%)
□ Continuity conversion rate (if applicable)
□ Email open rate (goal: 20-40% for cold, 30-50%+ for warm)
□ Email click rate (goal: 2-5% for cold, 5-15%+ for warm)
```

### Historical Data
```
□ Current benchmarks vs. previous period
□ Best-performing traffic source (what's working?)
□ Worst-performing step (where is the drop-off?)
□ A/B test history (what has already been tested?)
```

## Layer 1: Market Audit (Halbert Hierarchy)

> "Fix in this order: Market → Offer → Copy"

**Step 1: Market Check**

Is the product/offer targeting a hungry, reachable market?

- [ ] Is there demonstrated demand for this type of product? (Search volume, Amazon sales, competitor success)
- [ ] Are you targeting people who actively spend money in this category?
- [ ] Is the market reachable at a cost that allows profitability?
- [ ] Are you targeting the RIGHT starving crowd (buyers, not browsers)?

**Diagnosis:**
- Low traffic volume + low cost: Market too small
- High traffic cost + low conversion: Wrong market (non-buyers)
- High conversion but low volume: Good market, scale traffic

---

**Step 2: Offer Check**

Before any copy diagnosis, confirm the offer itself is competitive.

- [ ] Is the value-to-price ratio obvious to the prospect?
- [ ] Does the guarantee remove all perceived risk?
- [ ] Is the mechanism clearly differentiated from competitors?
- [ ] Are the bonuses relevant to the core problem?
- [ ] Is the price-point appropriate for the traffic temperature?

**Offer vs. Market sophistication matrix:**

| Market Sophistication | Required Offer Strategy |
|----------------------|------------------------|
| Level 1 (new market) | Just state the claim clearly |
| Level 2 (growing market) | Amplify the claim (stronger promise) |
| Level 3 (competitive) | Unique mechanism required |
| Level 4 (saturated) | Identity/tribe positioning required |
| Level 5 (oversaturated) | New sub-market or radical differentiation |

## Layer 2: CVO Structure Audit (Deiss Framework)

Map the funnel against the 7-step CVO model:

```
STEP 1: Product/Market Fit
   □ Is there validated demand for this offer?
   □ Diagnosis: YES / NO / UNCLEAR

STEP 2: Traffic Source
   □ Is traffic temperature matched to offer entry point?
   □ Cold traffic → Lead Magnet or Tripwire entry (not Core Offer)
   □ Diagnosis: MATCHED / MISMATCHED

STEP 3: Lead Magnet
   □ Does the lead magnet promise ONE specific outcome?
   □ Does it attract BUYERS not just free-seekers?
   □ Does it set up the Tripwire naturally?
   □ Opt-in rate: [X]% — Target: 20-40%+
   □ Diagnosis: PASS / WEAK / ABSENT

STEP 4: Tripwire
   □ Does the Tripwire exist?
   □ Is the value-to-price gap obvious and dramatic?
   □ Does the Tripwire page include an order bump?
   □ Tripwire conversion: [X]% — Target: 5-15% of opt-ins
   □ Diagnosis: PASS / WEAK / ABSENT

STEP 5: Core Offer
   □ Is the Core Offer presented naturally after Tripwire?
   □ Does the Core Offer page address all objections?
   □ Is the guarantee bold and specific?
   □ Core Offer conversion: [X]% — Target: 1-3% of total traffic
   □ Diagnosis: PASS / WEAK / ABSENT

STEP 6: Profit Maximizers
   □ Is there at least one upsell after Core Offer?
   □ Is there a continuity/subscription option?
   □ Is there a high-ticket backend?
   □ Upsell conversion: [X]% — Target: 15-30% of buyers
   □ Diagnosis: PASS / WEAK / ABSENT

STEP 7: Return Path
   □ Is there an email sequence post-purchase?
   □ Is the sequence segmented by buyer vs. non-buyer?
   □ Is there a re-engagement campaign for dormant subscribers?
   □ Diagnosis: PASS / WEAK / ABSENT
```

## Layer 3: Awareness Stage Audit (Schwartz Framework)

For each funnel entry point, confirm the awareness match:

```
COLD TRAFFIC ENTRY POINT:
   □ Traffic awareness stage: [1-5 estimated]
   □ Copy calibration: Stage [1-5]
   □ Match: YES / NO

   If MISMATCH:
   → Copy is [X] stages above audience: Loses them early (too aggressive)
   → Copy is [X] stages below audience: Feels condescending (too basic)

EMAIL SEQUENCE:
   □ Does the sequence assume correct awareness level?
   □ Email 1 assumes: Stage [X]
   □ Subscriber likely at: Stage [X]
   □ Match: YES / NO
```

## Layer 4: Persuasion Audit (Cialdini 7 Principles)

Score each principle 0-3 in the funnel:
- 0 = Not present
- 1 = Present but weak
- 2 = Present and adequate
- 3 = Present and strong

```
PRINCIPLE 1: RECIPROCITY
   □ Is genuine value given before any ask?
   □ Is the value unexpected and meaningful?
   Score: [0-3]
   Notes:

PRINCIPLE 2: COMMITMENT & CONSISTENCY
   □ Does the funnel have a yes-ladder (small → big commitments)?
   □ Does each step feel like the natural next consistent action?
   Score: [0-3]
   Notes:

PRINCIPLE 3: SOCIAL PROOF
   □ Are testimonials specific (name, result, timeframe)?
   □ Are they placed at the right moments of doubt?
   □ Quantity AND quality?
   Score: [0-3]
   Notes:

PRINCIPLE 4: AUTHORITY
   □ Is authority evidence present?
   □ Is it relevant to the specific claims made?
   □ Is the source credible to this specific audience?
   Score: [0-3]
   Notes:

PRINCIPLE 5: LIKING
   □ Does the copy persona feel authentic and likable?
   □ Is copy written in the prospect's language (VOC)?
   □ Is there genuine vulnerability/authenticity?
   Score: [0-3]
   Notes:

PRINCIPLE 6: SCARCITY
   □ Is there an urgency/scarcity element?
   □ Is it real and verifiable (not fake countdown)?
   □ Is the consequence of delay clear?
   Score: [0-3]
   Notes:

PRINCIPLE 7: UNITY
   □ Does the funnel create shared identity?
   □ Does it speak to "us vs. them" in a legitimate way?
   □ Does the prospect feel they BELONG with this brand?
   Score: [0-3]
   Notes:

TOTAL PERSUASION SCORE: [X] / 21
```

## Layer 5: Copy Quality Audit

### Headline Audit
For each major funnel page:

- [ ] Does the headline match the awareness stage?
- [ ] Is the headline specific (promise, result, timeframe)?
- [ ] Does it select the right prospect?
- [ ] Does it create urgency to read on?

### Body Copy Audit
- [ ] Reading level: 6th-8th grade?
- [ ] Sentence length: average under 15 words?
- [ ] "You" count vs "we/I" count — does "you" win?
- [ ] Are all claims specific (numbers, names, dates)?
- [ ] Is there passive voice? (Red flag: "results may be seen", "it has been shown")

### Offer Clarity Audit
- [ ] Can someone read the offer section in 60 seconds and know exactly what they get?
- [ ] Is the price clear?
- [ ] Is the guarantee obvious?
- [ ] Is the CTA specific ("Click the yellow button below")?

---

## Layer 6: Technical Audit

- [ ] Page load speed: Under 3 seconds? (Use GTmetrix or PageSpeed Insights)
- [ ] Mobile conversion: Is mobile conversion rate within 20% of desktop?
- [ ] Form friction: Does the opt-in form ask for minimal information?
- [ ] Checkout friction: How many steps to purchase? (Target: 1-2 max)
- [ ] Email deliverability: Open rates indicate good/poor deliverability?
