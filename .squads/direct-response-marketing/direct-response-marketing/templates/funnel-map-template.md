## 🚨 CONVERSION LEAK CHECKLIST

Run through this checklist monthly to find hidden leaks:

**Traffic:**
- [ ] CPC increasing? (audience fatigue, creative fatigue)
- [ ] CTR decreasing? (ad creative needs refresh)
- [ ] High bounce rate on landing page? (traffic/page mismatch)

**Opt-In Page:**
- [ ] Conversion rate below 30%? (weak headline, too much friction)
- [ ] Mobile conversion lower than desktop? (mobile UX issues)
- [ ] Exit rate high? (add exit popup)

**Sales Page:**
- [ ] Scroll depth under 60%? (copy too long or boring)
- [ ] High time on page but low conversion? (confused, not convinced)
- [ ] Drop-off at specific section? (heatmap analysis needed)

**Checkout:**
- [ ] Cart abandonment above 30%? (add abandoned cart emails)
- [ ] Form field abandonment? (simplify form)
- [ ] Payment errors? (technical issue, test checkout)

**Upsells:**
- [ ] Take rate below 15%? (offer not relevant or too expensive)
- [ ] Video completion low? (video too long, not compelling)

**Emails:**
- [ ] Open rate below 30%? (subject lines weak, list cold)
- [ ] Click rate below 10%? (CTAs weak, not enough value)
- [ ] Unsubscribe spike? (too frequent, not valuable)
