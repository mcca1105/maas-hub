## 📧 SOAP OPERA SEQUENCE (5-7 Days)
**Purpose:** Nurture new subscribers, build relationship, open loops, create anticipation
**Use Case:** New list members, post-opt-in, product launch buildup
**Cadence:** Daily
**Russell Brunson's Signature Framework**

---

### Email 1: SET THE STAGE (Day 0 - Immediate)

**Subject Line Options:**
- "Here's your [LEAD MAGNET]... (+ important question)"
- "[NAME], this changed everything for me"
- "You're in! (But first, quick question...)"

**Purpose:** Deliver lead magnet, set expectations, introduce character, open first loop

```
Subject: Here's your [LEAD MAGNET]... (+ important question)

Hey [FIRST_NAME],

Awesome! Your [LEAD MAGNET] is ready.

[LINK TO DOWNLOAD/ACCESS]

Before you dive in, I have a quick question...

[OPEN LOOP - Ask provocative question that highlights their pain/desire]

Example: "Have you ever wondered why some people seem to [ACHIEVE RESULT] effortlessly
while others struggle for years doing the exact same things?"

I'll be honest... I used to be in the second group.

And tomorrow, I'm going to share the exact moment everything changed for me.

It's a story I don't share often, but it completely shifted how I [APPROACH/THINK ABOUT X].

Watch for my email tomorrow - you won't want to miss it.

[YOUR NAME]

P.S. Make sure you [ACTION WITH LEAD MAGNET] today. There's a reason [SPECIFIC PART]
is on page [X] - it's the foundation for what I'm sharing with you this week.
```

**Elements:**
- ✅ Deliver on promise (lead magnet)
- ✅ Set expectations (more value coming)
- ✅ Open loop (unanswered question)
- ✅ Personal story teaser
- ✅ CTA to consume lead magnet

## 📋 EMAIL SEQUENCE BEST PRACTICES

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters (mobile)
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Use personalization sparingly ([[FIRST_NAME]] max 1 per 5 emails)
- Test questions vs statements
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, !!!, $$$, ALL CAPS)

### Open Loops
- Every email should close one loop and open another
- Keep them wanting more
- Pay off loops promptly (within 1-2 emails)
- Use cliffhangers strategically

### CTAs
- One primary CTA per email
- Clear what happens when they click
- Benefit-focused link text (not "click here")
- Multiple CTAs okay for long emails (every 3-4 paragraphs)

### Length
- Indoctrination: 200-400 words
- Sales: 300-600 words (can go longer if story-driven)
- Mobile-friendly: short paragraphs, white space

### Timing
- Send when your audience is most active (test)
- Consistent send times (condition opens)
- Daily for launches/sequences, 2-3x/week for nurture

### Personalization
- Use merge tags: [[FIRST_NAME]], [[COMPANY]], etc.
- Segment based on behavior
- Reference past actions (you downloaded X, you attended Y)

### Testing
- A/B test subject lines (biggest impact on opens)
- Test CTAs (biggest impact on clicks)
- Test send times
- Track: Open rate, click rate, unsubscribe rate
