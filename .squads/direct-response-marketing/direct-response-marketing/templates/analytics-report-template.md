**LTV Calculation:**
```
LTV = AOV × Average Purchase Frequency × Average Customer Lifespan

Example:
LTV = $150 (AOV) × 3 (purchases) × 365 days = $450
```

**LTV:CAC Target:** 3:1 minimum, 5:1 healthy, 8:1+ excellent
