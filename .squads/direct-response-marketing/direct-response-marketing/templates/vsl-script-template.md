# VSL Script Template
## Complete Video Sales Letter Script with Timing

**Product/Offer:** [PRODUCT NAME]
**Target Audience:** [AVATAR]
**Big Idea:** [THE CORE TRANSFORMATIVE CONCEPT]
**Unique Mechanism:** [WHAT MAKES THIS DIFFERENT]
**Total Duration:** 20-25 minutes

---

## 🎯 PATTERN INTERRUPT (0:00 - 0:15)
**Goal:** Stop the scroll, break pattern, create immediate curiosity

```
[VISUAL: Bold statement or unexpected image]

"Wait... stop scrolling for just a second."
OR
"If you [DESIRE] but [STRUGGLE], this might be the most important thing you see today."
OR
"What I'm about to show you is [CONTROVERSIAL/UNEXPECTED]..."

[TIMING: 5-15 seconds maximum]
```

**Notes:**
- Must break the pattern of what they're seeing
- Can be controversial, unusual, or provocative
- Sets the tone for the rest of the VSL

---

## 🎣 HOOK (0:15 - 0:45)
**Goal:** Create massive curiosity, make a promise, establish credibility

```
"In the next [X] minutes, I'm going to show you [SPECIFIC RESULT]...

WITHOUT [PAIN POINT 1]...
WITHOUT [PAIN POINT 2]...
AND WITHOUT [PAIN POINT 3]...

Even if [OBJECTION/PAST FAILURE].

I know that sounds [too good to be true/crazy/impossible], but stay with me, because
[PROOF ELEMENT - number, testimonial, statistic].

My name is [NAME], and [CREDIBILITY STATEMENT]."

[TIMING: 30-45 seconds total]
```

**Hook Checklist:**
- [ ] Specific promise (not vague)
- [ ] Address 3 common pain points
- [ ] Handle biggest objection upfront
- [ ] Quick credibility builder
- [ ] Create curiosity gap

---

## 📖 STORY SETUP (0:45 - 3:00)
**Goal:** Build rapport, show you understand their pain, make them relate

```
"Look, I get it. You've probably [COMMON STRUGGLE].

You've tried [FAILED SOLUTION 1]... didn't work.
You've tried [FAILED SOLUTION 2]... waste of time.
You've even tried [FAILED SOLUTION 3]... maybe saw some results but couldn't sustain it.

And now you're thinking... [INTERNAL DIALOGUE - doubt, frustration, resignation].

I know because I was there too.

[YOUR ORIGIN STORY - relatable struggle, rock bottom moment, 2-3 sentences]

I remember [SPECIFIC PAINFUL MOMENT]. I felt [EMOTION]. I thought [LIMITING BELIEF].

And that's when I realized... [EPIPHANY BRIDGE - what you discovered]."

[TIMING: 2-3 minutes]
```

**Story Elements:**
- Relatable character (you or client)
- Specific struggle (not generic)
- Failed attempts (3 minimum)
- Rock bottom moment
- Emotional connection
- Epiphany that led to discovery

---

## 😤 STRUGGLE & AGITATION (3:00 - 5:00)
**Goal:** Intensify pain, show why traditional solutions fail, build desire for alternative

```
"Here's what nobody tells you about [TRADITIONAL SOLUTION]...

[REVELATION 1 - why it doesn't work]
[REVELATION 2 - hidden downside]
[REVELATION 3 - why it's not your fault]

The truth is, the reason you've struggled with [PROBLEM] isn't because you're [SELF-BLAME].

It's because [EXTERNAL ENEMY - industry, myth, outdated method].

See, [EXPLANATION OF WHY TRADITIONAL APPROACHES FAIL].

And that's exactly why [NEW APPROACH] works when everything else fails."

[TIMING: 2 minutes]
```

**Agitation Triggers:**
- External enemy (not their fault)
- Industry lies/myths
- Outdated methods
- Hidden downsides of alternatives
- Statistics that shock

---

## 💡 DISCOVERY & MECHANISM REVEAL (5:00 - 8:00)
**Goal:** Introduce the unique mechanism, explain why it's different, create fascination

```
"So here's what I discovered...

[THE MOMENT OF DISCOVERY - story of how you found this]

It turns out, [UNIQUE MECHANISM EXPLANATION].

Instead of [OLD WAY], this works by [NEW WAY].

The difference is [KEY DIFFERENTIATOR].

[OPTIONAL: Scientific backing, research, authority citation]

Let me show you exactly how this works...

[3-STEP PROCESS]
Step 1: [ACTION] → [RESULT]
Step 2: [ACTION] → [RESULT]
Step 3: [ACTION] → [RESULT]

And when you combine these three steps, [TRANSFORMATION].

[TIMING: 3 minutes]
```

**Mechanism Checklist:**
- [ ] Unique and differentiated
- [ ] Simple to understand (3 steps max)
- [ ] Contrasts with old/failed methods
- [ ] Scientific or authoritative backing
- [ ] Visual if possible (diagram, chart)

---

## 🔬 PROOF & VALIDATION (8:00 - 12:00)
**Goal:** Prove it works, show social proof, build belief

```
"Now, I know what you're thinking... 'Does this actually work?'

Let me show you...

[TESTIMONIAL 1 - Video preferred, with specific result]
'[NAME] from [LOCATION] [ACHIEVED RESULT] in just [TIMEFRAME].'

[TESTIMONIAL 2 - Different avatar/use case]
'[NAME], who [SITUATION], now [RESULT].'

[TESTIMONIAL 3 - Handles objection]
'Even [NAME], who [OBSTACLE], was able to [RESULT].'

In fact, we've helped [NUMBER] people [ACHIEVE RESULT].

[SHOW PROOF ELEMENTS]
- Screenshots of results
- Before/after comparisons
- Data/statistics
- Media mentions
- Expert endorsements

[TIMING: 4 minutes]
```

**Proof Stack:**
- Minimum 3 testimonials (video > text)
- Variety of avatars/situations
- Specific numbers and results
- Visual proof (screenshots, charts)
- Authority/media mentions

---

## 🎁 THE OFFER (12:00 - 16:00)
**Goal:** Present what they get, stack value, make price irrelevant

```
"Okay, so you're probably wondering... how do you get access to this?

Well, I've put together [PRODUCT NAME] - and here's everything you're getting:

[CORE OFFER]
✅ [MAIN COMPONENT] - Normally $[VALUE], includes [SPECIFIC DETAILS]

[BONUS 1]
✅ [BONUS NAME] - $[VALUE] - [BENEFIT/WHAT IT DOES]

[BONUS 2]
✅ [BONUS NAME] - $[VALUE] - [BENEFIT/WHAT IT DOES]

[BONUS 3]
✅ [BONUS NAME] - $[VALUE] - [BENEFIT/WHAT IT DOES]

[FAST ACTION BONUS - if applicable]
✅ [BONUS NAME] - $[VALUE] - BUT ONLY if you [ACTION] in the next [TIMEFRAME]

All together, that's a total value of $[TOTAL VALUE].

But you're not going to pay $[TOTAL VALUE].
Not even half that.

Your investment today is just [PAYMENT OPTIONS].

That's [COMPARISON - less than coffee per day, etc.].

[TIMING: 4 minutes]
```

**Value Stack Checklist:**
- [ ] Core offer clearly defined
- [ ] 3+ bonuses (complementary value)
- [ ] Each item assigned dollar value
- [ ] Total value shown
- [ ] Actual price revealed (10x+ value gap)
- [ ] Payment options presented
- [ ] Value comparison (make price feel small)

---

## 🛡️ GUARANTEE (16:00 - 17:00)
**Goal:** Remove risk, reverse objection, make no-brainer

```
"And here's the best part...

You're protected by my [TIMEFRAME] [GUARANTEE NAME].

Here's how it works:

[SPECIFIC GUARANTEE TERMS]

If [CONDITION], simply [ACTION] and [REFUND/REMEDY].

No questions asked. No hassles. No hard feelings.

Which means you have [TIMEFRAME] to [BENEFIT] with ZERO risk.

So really, the only way you lose is if you don't try this.

[TIMING: 1 minute]
```

**Guarantee Types:**
- Money-back guarantee (30/60/90 day)
- Results guarantee (or refund)
- Better-than-money-back (keep bonuses)
- Performance guarantee

---

## ⏰ URGENCY & SCARCITY (17:00 - 18:00)
**Goal:** Create reason to act NOW

```
"Now, I need to be straight with you...

[URGENCY REASON - why they can't wait]

[SCARCITY ELEMENT]
We're only [LIMITING FACTOR - spots, time, quantity]...

And [CONSEQUENCE OF WAITING].

So if you're seeing this right now, [INSTRUCTION].

[COUNTDOWN TIMER OR DEADLINE]

[TIMING: 1 minute]
```

**Urgency/Scarcity Options:**
- Limited spots (enrollment cap)
- Limited time (deadline)
- Limited quantity (stock)
- Price increase (going up)
- Bonuses expiring
- Seasonal/event-based

---

## 🎯 CALL TO ACTION (18:00 - 19:00)
**Goal:** Tell them exactly what to do next

```
"Here's what to do right now:

Step 1: Click the button below that says '[CTA TEXT]'
Step 2: [NEXT ACTION - fill out form, enter payment, etc.]
Step 3: [WHAT HAPPENS - instant access, email, etc.]

[SHOW THE BUTTON/FORM]

And remember, you're protected by [GUARANTEE], so there's zero risk.

The only question is... are you ready to [DESIRED OUTCOME]?

If yes, click below now.

[TIMING: 1 minute]
```

**CTA Best Practices:**
- Crystal clear instructions (3 steps)
- Benefit-focused button text
- Multiple CTA opportunities
- Visual button/form
- Reminder of guarantee
- Final emotional appeal

---

## 📝 RECAP & FINAL CLOSE (19:00 - 20:00)
**Goal:** Remind them of everything, handle final objections, close strong

```
"Just to recap what you're getting today:

✅ [MAIN OFFER]
✅ [BONUS 1]
✅ [BONUS 2]
✅ [BONUS 3]
✅ [GUARANTEE]

All for just [PRICE].

Plus, remember [URGENCY - deadline, scarcity].

So click the button below right now to [OUTCOME].

I'll see you on the inside.

[YOUR NAME]

P.S. [FINAL HOOK - address lingering objection, restate biggest benefit, or create FOMO]

[TIMING: 1 minute]
```

## ⚙️ PRODUCTION NOTES

### Timing Breakdown
- Total: 20-25 minutes (sweet spot for conversion)
- Hook: First 45 seconds (critical)
- Content: 45 sec - 17 min (story + mechanism + proof + offer)
- Close: 17-20 min (guarantee + urgency + CTA)

### Pattern Interrupts
Insert every 2-3 minutes:
- Visual change
- "Here's the weird part..."
- "But wait, there's more..."
- Testimonial cutaway
- Shocking statistic
- Question to audience

### Captions
- ALWAYS include captions (80% watch muted)
- Bold key phrases
- Highlight numbers and benefits

### Visual Guidelines
- Hook: Bold text or unusual visual
- Story: Relatable images/B-roll
- Mechanism: Diagrams, charts, visual explanation
- Proof: Testimonial videos, before/after
- Offer: Product shots, value stack visual
- CTA: Big obvious button

### Mobile Optimization
- Large text (readable on small screens)
- Vertical format (9:16) or square (1:1)
- Captions always visible
- CTA button thumb-friendly
