# Long-Form Sales Letter Template

**Product/Offer:** [PRODUCT NAME]
**Target Audience:** [AVATAR]
**Awareness Stage:** [1-5 per Schwartz]
**Market Sophistication:** [1-5]
**Big Idea:** [THE TRANSFORMATIVE CONCEPT]

## 🎯 HEADLINE
**Purpose:** Stop them dead, promise transformation, create curiosity

**Format Options:**

### How-To Formula
```
"How [SPECIFIC AVATAR] Can [DESIRED RESULT]
Without [PAIN/OBSTACLE 1], [PAIN/OBSTACLE 2], or [PAIN/OBSTACLE 3]"

Example:
"How Busy Entrepreneurs Can Build a 6-Figure Funnel
Without Expensive Developers, Technical Skills, or Wasting Months"
```

### Discovery Formula
```
"Discover the [UNIQUE MECHANISM] That [SPECIFIC RESULT]
in Just [TIMEFRAME]... Even If [MAJOR OBJECTION]"

Example:
"Discover the 'Invisible Funnel' That Generated $2.3M in 90 Days...
Even If You've Never Built a Funnel Before"
```

### Secret Formula
```
"The [ADJECTIVE] Secret [AUTHORITY] Uses to [AMAZING RESULT]
That [BIG COMPANY/COMPETITOR] Doesn't Want You to Know"

Example:
"The Underground Method Top 1% Copywriters Use to Command $25K Per Letter
That Upwork Gurus Pray You Never Discover"
```

### Warning Formula
```
"Warning: [THREAT] Unless You [PROTECTIVE ACTION]"

Example:
"Warning: Your Funnel Is Bleeding 73% of Your Profits Unless You Fix These 3 Invisible Leaks"
```

**Headline Checklist:**
- [ ] Specific (not vague)
- [ ] Benefit-driven (what they get)
- [ ] Curiosity-inducing (must read more)
- [ ] Credible (not too hyped)
- [ ] Audience-targeted (for them specifically)

## 📝 DECK COPY / SUBHEADLINE
**Purpose:** Expand on promise, add specificity, increase credibility

```
"[EXPAND ON PROMISE]... Discover the proven [METHOD/SYSTEM] that
[SPECIFIC RESULT WITH NUMBERS] used by [AUTHORITY/NUMBER OF PEOPLE]..."

AND/OR

"[TIMEFRAME PROMISE]: Follow this simple [X]-step [SYSTEM] and
[SPECIFIC OUTCOME] in as little as [TIMEFRAME], guaranteed."
```

## 🚀 LEAD / OPENING (First 100-150 words)
**Purpose:** Hook them hard, establish relatability, build curiosity

**Classic Gary Halbert Lead:**
```
"Dear [AVATAR],

Let me ask you a question: [PROVOCATIVE QUESTION THAT HIGHLIGHTS THEIR PAIN]?

If you answered yes... then what I'm about to share might be the most [IMPACTFUL]
thing you read this [year/month/week].

Here's why:

[SHOCKING STATISTIC or BOLD CLAIM]

And in the next [X] minutes, I'm going to show you exactly how to [PROMISE]
without [PAIN 1], [PAIN 2], or [PAIN 3].

In fact, [PROOF TEASER - mention specific result, person, or number].

Sound impossible?

Keep reading, because I'm about to prove it..."
```

**Lead Checklist:**
- [ ] Addresses reader directly ("you")
- [ ] Asks provocative question
- [ ] Makes bold promise
- [ ] Teases proof
- [ ] Creates open loop (must keep reading)

## 😤 PROBLEM AGITATION
**Purpose:** Make them FEEL the pain before presenting solution

```
"Look, I get it.

You've probably tried [FAILED SOLUTION 1]. Didn't work.
Maybe you invested in [FAILED SOLUTION 2]. Waste of money.
You might have even [FAILED SOLUTION 3]. Some results, but couldn't sustain it.

And now you're thinking... [INTERNAL DIALOGUE - resignation, doubt, frustration].

I know because [CREDIBILITY - I was there / I've helped X people who were there].

Here's what's really going on:

[REVELATION 1 - Why traditional solutions fail]
[REVELATION 2 - The hidden enemy/myth]
[REVELATION 3 - Why it's not their fault]

The truth is, [EXTERNAL BLAME - industry, outdated methods, myths].

And that's exactly why [NEW APPROACH] works when everything else fails."
```

**Agitation Elements:**
- Failed attempts (3+)
- External enemy (not their fault)
- Industry lies/myths
- Hidden costs of inaction
- Emotional language

## ✨ FASCINATION BULLETS
**Purpose:** Create irresistible curiosity, tease specific benefits without revealing how

**Eugene Schwartz Bullet Formula:**

```
"Here's just a small sample of what you'll discover inside:

• The '[ADJECTIVE]' [METHOD] that [SPECIFIC RESULT]... and why [REASON IT WORKS] (page XX)

• Why [COUNTERINTUITIVE FACT] actually [UNEXPECTED BENEFIT]... even though [COMMON BELIEF]

• The [NUMBER]-[TIMEFRAME] [TECHNIQUE] that [AMAZING RESULT]... works even if [OBJECTION]

• [AUTHORITY]'s secret for [RESULT] that [BIG COMPANY] spent $[AMOUNT] to [ACTION]... yours free on page XX

• The '[NICKNAME]' method for [BENEFIT] - takes just [SHORT TIME] and [SURPRISING EASE]

• WARNING: Never [COMMON ACTION] until you read page XX... here's why [DANGER/LOSS]

• The 'backwards' approach to [PROBLEM] that [RESULT] while [SURPRISING BENEFIT]... sounds crazy but [PROOF]

• How to [DESIRED OUTCOME] without [PAIN 1], [PAIN 2], or even [PAIN 3]... see page XX

• The [SPECIFIC THING] I discovered [PLACE/SITUATION] that [UNEXPECTED OUTCOME]... (the story alone is worth the price)

• [NUMBER] words you must NEVER use when [SITUATION]... or you'll [NEGATIVE CONSEQUENCE]

Plus [NUMBER] more [BENEFITS/SECRETS/METHODS] I'll share inside..."
```

**Bullet Checklist:**
- [ ] 10-20 bullets minimum
- [ ] Specific (numbers, names, details)
- [ ] Curiosity gap (tease, don't tell)
- [ ] Page numbers (creates tangibility)
- [ ] Varied structure (not repetitive)
- [ ] "Reason why" included
- [ ] Counterintuitive when possible

## 🛡️ GUARANTEE (Risk Reversal)
**Purpose:** Eliminate all risk, make decision no-brainer

```
"And just in case you're still on the fence...

You're protected by my [TIMEFRAME] [GUARANTEE NAME]:

[BOLD PROMISE]

Here's how it works:

[SPECIFIC TERMS - Try it, use it, test it for X days]

If [CONDITION - you're not satisfied, don't see results, don't love it]...

Simply [ACTION - email us, request refund, contact support]...

And I'll [REFUND/REMEDY - refund every penny, let you keep bonuses, etc.].

No questions asked.
No hassles.
No hard feelings.

Which means you have a full [TIMEFRAME] to [EXPERIENCE PRODUCT] with ZERO risk.

In fact, the way I see it... the ONLY way you can lose is by not trying this.

[OPTIONAL: Better-than-money-back guarantee]
"Actually, even if you decide [PRODUCT] isn't for you... keep [BONUS] as my gift
for giving it a shot. It's yours to keep either way."

Fair enough?
```

**Guarantee Options:**
- Standard money-back (30/60/90 days)
- Results guarantee (or refund)
- Better-than-money-back (keep bonuses)
- Conditional guarantee (if you do X and don't get Y)
- Lifetime guarantee
- Performance guarantee

## 📌 P.S. SECTION FORMULAS

**Many readers skip straight to the P.S. - make it count!**

### Formula 1: Remind of Guarantee
```
"P.S. Remember, you're protected by my [TIMEFRAME] [GUARANTEE]. Try [PRODUCT]
risk-free. If it doesn't [PROMISE], get every penny back. You literally can't lose."
```

### Formula 2: Urgency Reminder
```
"P.S. Don't forget - this offer expires [DATE/TIME]. After that, the price increases
to $[HIGHER PRICE] and you'll miss out on [BONUS]. Order now to lock in this special price."
```

### Formula 3: Final Objection Handling
```
"P.S. Still on the fence? I get it. But ask yourself: What's the cost of doing nothing?
If you're still [STRUGGLING] six months from now because you didn't take action today,
how will you feel? The only way to lose is by not trying."
```

### Formula 4: Social Proof Reminder
```
"P.S. Don't just take my word for it - [NUMBER] people have already ordered [PRODUCT]
and are seeing [RESULTS]. Join them by clicking the button above."
```

## 📋 PRE-PUBLISH CHECKLIST

Before going live with your sales letter:

**Headline & Lead**
- [ ] Headline tested (10+ variations minimum)
- [ ] Subheadline expands on promise
- [ ] First 100 words hook hard
- [ ] Open loop created (must keep reading)

**Body Copy**
- [ ] Problem agitated (emotional pain)
- [ ] Big Idea clearly presented
- [ ] Unique mechanism explained
- [ ] 10+ fascination bullets
- [ ] Bullets create curiosity (don't reveal how)

**Proof**
- [ ] 3+ detailed testimonials
- [ ] Specific results (numbers, timeframes)
- [ ] Before/after comparisons
- [ ] Authority/media mentions

**Offer**
- [ ] Value stack shown
- [ ] Total value is 10x+ actual price
- [ ] Payment options clear
- [ ] Bonuses are valuable (not fluff)

**Risk Reversal**
- [ ] Strong guarantee (30+ days minimum)
- [ ] Guarantee terms crystal clear
- [ ] Risk completely eliminated

**Urgency**
- [ ] Genuine reason for limitation
- [ ] Deadline or scarcity element
- [ ] Consequence of waiting shown

**CTA**
- [ ] Clear 3-step instructions
- [ ] Benefit-focused button text
- [ ] Multiple CTAs throughout (every 500-700 words)
- [ ] Final CTA impossible to miss

**Technical**
- [ ] Mobile-friendly formatting
- [ ] Fast load time (under 3 seconds)
- [ ] No spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] All links work
- [ ] Checkout process tested
- [ ] Thank you page ready

**P.S. Section**
- [ ] P.S. included (many skip to this)
- [ ] Restates main benefit or urgency
- [ ] Final CTA button below P.S.
