# Gary Halbert - Classic Sales Letter Example

## Context
Gary Halbert is considered one of the greatest copywriters of all time. His "Boron Letters" written to his son from prison are the most valuable copywriting education ever created.

**Core Philosophy:** "List (Market) > Offer > Copy"
- The most important element is WHO you're selling to (starving crowd)
- Second is WHAT you're offering them
- Copy is third (but still crucial)

---

## Classic Sales Letter Structure

### PRE-HEADLINE (Above the main headline)
```
FREE REPORT REVEALS...
```
or
```
ANNOUNCING...
```

**Purpose:** Pattern interrupt, creates curiosity

---

### HEADLINE (Biggest, boldest text)

**Halbert's Formula:**
1. **Identify the starving crowd**
2. **Promise the food they crave**

**Examples from Halbert:**

```
"How To Burn Off Body Fat Hour-By-Hour!"
```
- WHO: People wanting to lose weight (starving crowd)
- WHAT: Specific mechanism (hour-by-hour fat burning)
- Benefit: Clear outcome

```
"Amazing Secret Discovered By One-Legged Golfer
Adds 50 Yards To Your Drives, Eliminates Hooks and Slices...
And Can Slash Up To 10 Strokes From Your Game Almost Overnight!"
```
- WHO: Golfers (starving crowd for improvement)
- WHAT: Secret from unlikely source (credibility + curiosity)
- RESULTS: Specific numbers (50 yards, 10 strokes)
- TIME: Almost overnight (fast results)

---

### LEAD (First 200-300 words)

**Halbert's Approach:**
1. Expand on the headline promise
2. Agitate the problem
3. Hint at the unique solution
4. Build credibility fast

**Example Structure:**

```
Dear Friend,

If you're like most golfers, you've spent hundreds (maybe thousands)
of dollars on new clubs, lessons, and training aids... only to see
your handicap stay exactly the same.

Frustrating, isn't it?

Well, I've got some good news for you. There's a simple adjustment
you can make to your swing (takes about 4 minutes to learn) that can
add 50+ yards to your drives and dramatically improve your accuracy.

Sound too good to be true?

It did to me too, until I met a one-legged golfer named [Name] who
showed me this technique and I immediately added 63 yards to my drive.

Here's the story...
```

**What Makes This Work:**
- ✅ Speaks to the pain (wasted money, no results)
- ✅ Promises specific solution (simple adjustment)
- ✅ Overcomes skepticism (I was skeptical too)
- ✅ Proof element (one-legged golfer, 63 yards)
- ✅ Opens a loop (here's the story)

### BODY COPY

**Halbert's "Starving Crowd" Letter Structure:**

**1. Story-Based Opening (300-500 words)**
```
Let me tell you about the day I discovered this...

I was at [location], feeling [emotion], when [inciting incident].

That's when I met [character] who showed me [solution].

At first, I was skeptical because [objection].

But then [proof moment] happened and I knew this was real.
```

**2. The Problem (Agitate) (200-300 words)**
```
You see, the reason most golfers never improve is because [false belief].

The golf industry has convinced you that [mainstream solution] is the answer.

But here's the truth they don't want you to know...

[Real problem revealed]

This is why you've been struggling, not because [it's not your fault framing].
```

**3. The Solution (Mechanism) (400-600 words)**
```
What I discovered is that [unique mechanism].

Unlike [old way] which requires [barrier], this method [benefit].

Here's how it works:

[Step 1: Simple explanation]
[Step 2: Simple explanation]
[Step 3: Simple explanation]

The reason this works is because [scientific or logical explanation].

[Expert authority] says, "[Quote supporting this]"
```

**4. Proof & Results (300-500 words)**
```
Since I started using this method:

• My drive increased from 230 yards to 293 yards
• My handicap dropped from 18 to 12
• I won the club championship for the first time

But I'm not the only one...

[Testimonial 1 - Specific results]
"I added 47 yards in just 3 days" - John S., Phoenix

[Testimonial 2 - Specific results]
"My slice completely disappeared" - Mary T., Dallas

[Testimonial 3 - Specific results]
"I'm hitting fairways 80% of the time now" - Bob R., Seattle

Over 12,347 golfers have used this method with an average increase of 43 yards.
```

### FASCINATION BULLETS (Halbert's Secret Weapon)

**Structure:**
```
Inside you'll discover:

• How a one-legged golfer accidentally discovered this technique
  while recovering from surgery (the story will amaze you!)

• The "forbidden" grip adjustment that the PGA doesn't want you to
  know about (page 23)

• Why your expensive titanium driver is actually HURTING your game
  (this shocked me!)

• The 4-minute pre-shot routine used by tour pros that adds 30+
  yards to every drive (you can do this starting today)

• What Arnold Palmer told me about this technique when I showed it
  to him at [tournament] (his exact words on page 47)

• The "sweet spot secret" that 99% of golfers miss (once you see
  this, you'll never mis-hit again)
```

**What Makes Great Bullets:**
- ✅ Specific benefit (30+ yards)
- ✅ Curiosity gap (you don't reveal everything)
- ✅ Proof element (Arnold Palmer, page 47)
- ✅ Unique mechanism (sweet spot secret)
- ✅ Emotional hook (shocked me, forbidden, doesn't want you to know)

**Halbert's Rule:** 20+ bullets minimum, with at least 10 being "irresistible"

### THE OFFER

**Halbert's Formula:**
1. **Price anchoring** - Show the real value first
2. **Dramatic discount** - Make price seem tiny
3. **Bonuses** - Stack value
4. **Guarantee** - Reverse risk completely

**Example:**

```
Here's What You Get:

✓ The Complete "50 Yards More" System (Value: $297)
✓ Video Demonstrations of Every Technique (Value: $147)
✓ Personalized Swing Analysis Template (Value: $97)
✓ 30-Day "Lower Handicap" Guarantee (Value: Priceless)

TOTAL VALUE: $541

YOUR PRICE TODAY: Just $47

But wait... there's more:

ORDER NOW and I'll also include:

BONUS #1: "The Mental Game" Audio Course (Value: $77)
BONUS #2: "Course Management Secrets" eBook (Value: $47)
BONUS #3: Lifetime Updates & New Techniques (Value: $197)

NEW TOTAL VALUE: $862

YOUR PRICE: Still just $47

That's 94% off the real value!
```

**Why This Works:**
- ✅ Value stacking (shows what they get)
- ✅ Price anchoring ($541 vs $47 = bargain)
- ✅ Bonuses (builds perceived value)
- ✅ Scarcity implied (ORDER NOW)
- ✅ Math (94% off = rational justification)

## THE P.S. SECTION (Halbert's Secret Weapon)

**Why P.S. is Critical:**
- 📊 70%+ of people read the P.S. even if they skip the body
- 📊 P.S. is often the #2 most-read part after the headline
- 📊 A good P.S. can recover lost sales

**Halbert's P.S. Formula:**

**P.S. #1:** Restate main benefit + guarantee
**P.P.S.:** Add social proof or testimonial
**P.P.P.S.:** Final urgency reminder

## HALBERT'S COPY PRINCIPLES

### 1. The Starving Crowd Principle
"The most important element in direct mail is who you send it to, not what you send."

**Application:**
- Find people desperate for your solution
- Focus on markets with intense desire
- Example: New parents (sleep-deprived) > General parenting advice

### 2. The Grease Slide
"Make the first sentence so compelling they HAVE to read the second. Make the second so compelling they HAVE to read the third..."

**Application:**
- Short sentences
- Open loops constantly
- Never give them a reason to stop reading

### 3. The Hand-Raiser Method
"Get them to raise their hand before you sell them anything."

**Application:**
- Free report first
- Build list of interested prospects
- Then sell to qualified buyers

### 4. The Personal Touch
"Write like you're writing to one person - your friend."

**Application:**
- Use "you" and "I"
- Tell personal stories
- Be conversational

## WHAT NOT TO DO (Halbert's Warnings)

❌ **Don't Be Boring** - If it doesn't make you excited, rewrite it
❌ **Don't Be Vague** - Specific numbers beat generalities
❌ **Don't Hide the Offer** - Tell them what you're selling
❌ **Don't Forget the Guarantee** - Risk reversal is mandatory
❌ **Don't Skip Testing** - Always test headlines and offers
