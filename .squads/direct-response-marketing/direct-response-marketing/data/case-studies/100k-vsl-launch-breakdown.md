# Case Study: $100k VSL Launch in 30 Days

## Campaign Overview

**Product:** Digital course on email marketing
**Price Point:** $297
**Launch Date:** March 2025
**Total Revenue:** $112,847 in 30 days
**Units Sold:** 380
**Traffic Source:** Facebook Ads → VSL → Sales Page
**Total Ad Spend:** $14,230
**ROI:** 7.9x

---

## The Setup (Pre-Launch)

### Market Research (2 weeks)

**What We Did:**
1. **Mined Amazon reviews** - 200+ reviews from competing books
2. **Reddit research** - 50+ posts in r/EmailMarketing, r/entrepreneur
3. **Surveys** - Sent to existing list (500 subscribers)
4. **Competitor analysis** - Studied 10 similar courses

**Key Findings:**
- Pain Point #1: "I write emails but nobody opens them" (mentioned 67 times)
- Pain Point #2: "I don't know what to write about" (mentioned 54 times)
- Pain Point #3: "My list is small and not growing" (mentioned 48 times)
- Desired Outcome: "Consistent sales from email" (mentioned 89 times)
- Language: "crickets", "ghost town", "feels like I'm talking to myself"

**Critical Insight:**
They don't want to learn email marketing. They want to **stop feeling like they're screaming into the void**.

This became our mechanism.

---

## The Mechanism (Our Unique Approach)

Instead of "email marketing course", we positioned it as:

**"The Daily Email Method"**

**Promise:** Write one 5-minute email per day that makes sales while building a loyal audience.

**Why This Worked:**
- ✅ Specific (one email, daily, 5 minutes)
- ✅ Addresses time objection (just 5 minutes)
- ✅ Dual benefit (sales + engagement)
- ✅ Different from "email sequences" and "automation"

**The Hook:**
"The email I wrote in 4 minutes at a coffee shop made $2,847 the next day. Here's the template I used..."

## What Didn't Work (The Losses)

### 1. YouTube Ads (Wasted $711)
- CPM too high ($14 vs Facebook $3.50)
- Wrong audience (viewers, not buyers)
- Low intent
- **Decision: Cut after week 1**

### 2. Long-form Sales Page (Initial Version)
- 5,000 words, lots of detail
- Conversion rate: 3.2%
- People wanted to buy from VSL, not read more
- **Fix: Shortened to 1,500 words, CR jumped to 8.2%**

### 3. Broad Targeting (First 3 days)
- "Business owners" = too broad
- High CPC ($4.80)
- Low conversion (1.1%)
- **Fix: Narrowed to course creators, coaches = CPC dropped to $1.10**

### 4. Generic Ad Creative (First batch)
- "Learn email marketing" = boring
- Low CTR (0.8%)
- **Fix: Screenshot of $2,847 email = CTR jumped to 2.8%**

---

## Key Lessons

### 1. Research is 50% of Success
Spent 2 weeks on research. Best time invested.

### 2. Specific Numbers Beat Vague Claims
"Make money" < "$2,847 in one day"

### 3. Mechanism Matters
"Email course" = commodity
"Daily Email Method" = unique

### 4. VSL Does the Selling
Sales page should support, not re-sell

### 5. Scarcity Must Be Real
500-student limit was real. Could've sold 600+, but kept limit to maintain quality.

### 6. Guarantee Reduces Friction
60-day, revenue-based guarantee = removed biggest objection

### 7. Testimonials = Trust
15 case studies with real numbers = credibility

### 8. Test Small, Scale Fast
Week 1: Test ($2k)
Week 2-3: Scale winners ($8k)
Week 4: Maintain ($4k)
