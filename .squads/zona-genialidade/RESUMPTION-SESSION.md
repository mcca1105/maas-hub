## 📋 **What Happens Next**

### Phase 1: Intake (2 min)
Chief asks 3 contextual questions:
1. Qual é sua situação profissional hoje? (Empregado / Freelancer / Empreendedor / Em transição)
2. O que te trouxe ao Cohort Fundamentals? (Empreender / Pivotar / Escalar / Descobrir)
3. Se você pudesse resolver UMA coisa na sua vida profissional agora, qual seria?

### Phase 2: Assessment (30 min max)
43 unified questions that feed 7 frameworks simultaneously:
- **Contexto Pessoal** (5 min, ~8 perguntas)
- **Atividades e Energia** (8 min, ~12 perguntas)
- **Talentos e Padrões** (7 min, ~10 perguntas)
- **Estilo de Negócios** (5 min, ~8 perguntas)
- **Visão e Ambição** (5 min, ~5 perguntas)

### Phase 3: Analysis (Autonomous)
7 agents process your data in parallel:
- Gay Hendricks → Zone of Genius diagnosis
- Don Clifton → Top 5 strengths
- Dan Sullivan → Unique Ability mapping
- Roger Hamilton → Wealth profile & squad match
- Alex Hormozi → Monetization strategy
- Kathy Kolbe → Execution style
- Sally Hogshead → Positioning archetype

### Phase 4: Blueprint (Output)
Single comprehensive document:
1. **30-second profile** — Your essence
2. **Genius zone diagnosis** — Where you're unstoppable
3. **Strengths map** — Your top talents
4. **Unique ability** — What only you can do
5. **Wealth profile** — Your natural business style
6. **Recommended squad** — Which squad to build/run
7. **Monetization plan** — How to turn genius into revenue
8. **Execution style** — How you naturally work
9. **Positioning** — How the market sees you
10. **Next steps** — Concrete action plan

## 🔧 **Troubleshooting**

**If squad doesn't activate:**
1. Try: `/zona-genialidade start`
2. Try: `@zona-genialidade` (then `*start`)
3. Try: `@aiox-master *task zona-genialidade-start`

**If stuck:**
- The chief will ask clarifying questions
- You can always type `*help` to see commands
- Type `*exit` to pause and resume later
